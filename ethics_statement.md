# Ethics Statement: IntelliDrive AI

## Purpose
IntelliDrive AI is designed to demonstrate the potential of computer vision and reinforcement learning in autonomous driving. It is intended for research and educational purposes only.

## Safety & Responsibility
- **Simulation Environment**: This system operates in a simulated environment. Real-world deployment requires rigorous testing in controlled settings.
- **Human-in-the-loop**: We advocate for systems where human operators can intervene at any moment.
- **Fail-safe mechanisms**: The AI is programmed with priority rules (e.g., STOP signs always trigger BRAKE) that override learned behavior when confidence is high.

## Data Ethics
- The dataset used for training consists of public traffic sign data.
- No personal data or identifiable information from real drivers or pedestrians is stored or processed.

## Environmental Impact
- Training was optimized to minimize computational resources and carbon footprint.
